Grade 11 English — External Digital Assessment Information

1. Exam Overview

Welcome to the Square Country Ministry of Education’s digital assessment platform. This system is used for all external online assessments under the Square Country Qualifications Authority.

  • You will complete the exam entirely online using this secure interface.
  • Each question will appear on a separate page or tab within the exam.
  • Your responses will be automatically saved as you type.
  • You will have access to a timer, spell check, and answer review tools during the exam.

2. Rules & Monitoring

This digital assessment is conducted under secure examination conditions. By proceeding, you agree to the following:

  • Monitoring: Your screen activity, keyboard input, and mouse movement may be tracked to ensure exam integrity.
  • Security: Opening new tabs or navigating away from the exam may trigger an automatic warning, lock-out, or log-out.
  • Communication: You must not communicate with other candidates or use external devices during the exam.
  • Submission: Your responses will be securely encrypted and transmitted to SCQA upon submission.

3. Access Code

To begin your digital exam, please enter the 4-digit access code provided by your exam supervisor.

Incorrect access code. Please speak to your exam supervisor.
