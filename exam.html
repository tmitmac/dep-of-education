Grade 11 English — Standard 63021: Demonstrate understanding of synthesizing textual analysis across genres

Question 1 — Comparative Analysis (10 marks)

Compare how two different texts from distinct genres (e.g., a poem and a short story) explore a common theme such as identity, power, or belonging.

Question 2 — Synthesis and Evaluation (10 marks)

Explain how the use of style and structure across your selected texts contributes to the reader’s understanding of a shared idea.

Question 3 — Insight and Interpretation (10 marks)

Discuss how context (historical, cultural, or personal) influences the interpretation of ideas across your chosen genres.
